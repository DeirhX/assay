Rebalance planner

Drift of every targeted name vs its no-trade band, with the minimal band-closing trade sized in CZK. Edit any planned amount to simulate — the cash math and projected weights update live. Weights are % of the invested book (market value ÷ invested), matching rebalance.py --check. This is advice, not orders.

Portfolio risk lens

Single-name bands are blind to the real risk in a concentrated semis/AI book: the names move together. This shows correlation, effective number of independent bets, the share of variance that is pure co-movement, and a factor-shock stress test. It is a decision aid, not a risk oracle — read the caveats.

Decision journal

Log every decision with its thesis and price. Outcomes (your recorded price, or the live snapshot mark) calibrate whether your judgement actually works — a buy is "right" if it rose, a trim if it fell. Append-only; stored in your private data. Not advice.
